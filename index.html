A Comparison of Lexical Tokenization Methods

Honors research project. I compared several methods of tokenization in a simple sentiment analysis task.
Primarily, I wanted to compare morphological tokenization and Wordpiece tokenization, with the hypothesis that morphological tokenization would perform better.
I also looked at inserting tokens to indicate capitalization for non-case-sensitive tokenization methods.
The results were somewhat inconclusive. I think it was because the task is pretty simple; a more complex task (e.g. translation) could have better results.
